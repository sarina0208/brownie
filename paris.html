PARIS

History
A Tower Once Hated, Now Loved Worldwide

When it first rose above Paris in 1889, critics called it an eyesore. Today, the Eiffel Tower is a global icon and a symbol of French pride, standing tall as a timeless piece of history.

Architecture
A Giant of Iron and Imagination

Crafted from over 18,000 iron pieces, this towering structure was a bold leap in engineering. Gustave Eiffel didn’t just build a monument he redefined what was possible in architecture.

Exploration
Touch the Sky in the City of Lights

Ride the glass elevator or take the adventurous stairs—either way, the reward is unbeatable. From dazzling city views by day to twinkling lights by night, the Eiffel Tower is Paris at its most magical.
